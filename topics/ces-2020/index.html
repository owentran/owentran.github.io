Consmer Electronics Show

Every year, there's always chatter about the vast scale and distance walked to attend CES. The monolith approach has broken down, and requires some reengineering. Maybe it's time to shard the conference, hash the consumers to each bucket, or distribute into multiple regions. :)

Some of the more amusing products announced:

  • Ballie - a rolling ball that follows you around your home
  • Quibi has the worst name (short for Quick Bite), but encodes video with landscape that smartly zooms in for portrait mode
  • Charmin Robot to rescue you during TP emergencies
